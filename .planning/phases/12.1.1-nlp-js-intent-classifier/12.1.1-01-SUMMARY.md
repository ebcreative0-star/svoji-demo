# Phase 12.1.1 Plan 01: NLP.js Classifier Module and Training Corpus Summary

**One-liner:** NLP.js v4 singleton with Czech language support trained on 420 utterances across 21 intents, all 6 INTENT-0x criteria passing.

## What Was Built

### nlp-classifier.ts
Standalone NLP.js module with:
- `import 'server-only'` guard (prevents client/edge bundling)
- `NlpClassifier` class with lazy `init()` and `classify()` methods
- Module-level + `global._nlpClassifier` singleton for dev hot reload safety
- Correct `result.score` mapping (not `result.confidence`) per Pitfall 1 from research
- `getNlpClassifier()` factory export for use by Plan 02 integration

### training-data.json
NLP.js corpus format with:
- 21 intents (all required intent names matching `intent-classifier.ts` exactly)
- 420 total utterances (target was 150-200; exceeded due to comprehensive diacritics + plain variants)
- Each intent has 14-29 utterances covering: with diacritics, without diacritics, colloquial, different word order
- `small_talk` and `advice_request` have sufficient coverage to keep ambiguous short messages below 0.5 threshold
